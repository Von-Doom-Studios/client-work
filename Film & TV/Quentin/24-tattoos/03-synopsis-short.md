# SHORT SYNOPSIS

PIERRE SQUARE is a 29-year-old Uber Eats cyclist and failed actor, grinding through rainy Paris nights with nothing but bad luck and two pet fish to his name. After a disastrous sushi delivery crash-lands him at the birthday party of OLIVIER — an eccentric, reckless millionaire — Pierre wakes the next morning to find his host dead of an overdose.

Before calling the ambulance, he discovers Olivier's secret: the man tattooed his entire €6.2 million crypto seed phrase across his own body, hiding the fortune in ink. Pierre photographs the tattoos — 23 words and a mysterious constellation of dots — but loses the evidence when JULIA, a sharp and suspicious woman who was also at the party, accidentally deletes the photos.

What follows is a darkly comic, increasingly unhinged treasure hunt through Paris and the Normandy coast: an autopsy infiltration, a cemetery heist, a road trip to a dementia ward, and a revelation hidden in UV ink on the back of Julia's neck — placed there by Olivier himself, the man who trusted her with the key.

When they finally crack the wallet and find €6.2 million waiting, Pierre makes a choice that redefines what the whole chase was ever really about.

*24 TATTOOS* is a fast, funny, and unexpectedly moving film about bad luck, human decency, and the things worth more than money.
