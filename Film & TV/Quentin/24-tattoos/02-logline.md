# LOGLINE

A broke, perpetually unlucky aspiring actor stumbles onto a dead millionaire's body the morning after a party — and discovers the man tattooed a €6 million crypto fortune across his own skin. With the corpse cremated, the clock ticking, and the only other witness a sharp, suspicious woman with secrets of her own, he races to decode 24 tattoos before the trail — and the body — disappears forever.
