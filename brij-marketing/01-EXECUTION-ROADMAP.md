# Execution Roadmap — BRIJ Marketing

Two launches. Four weeks. $200/week boost budget.

---

## THE TWO CAMPAIGNS

**Campaign 1: "WE EVOLVED" — Espresso Cash → BRIJ (Week 1)**
Concept: This isn't a rebrand announcement. It's a transformation story. A wallet became an entire infrastructure layer. The narrative is ambition — a product outgrew its own name.

**Campaign 2: "THE FLIGHT THAT BOOKED ITSELF" — BRIJ Travel Launch (Weeks 2–4)**
Concept: The world's first AI-booked flight is a historic moment and we treat it like one. The hook is visceral: everyone hates booking flights. Everyone. We don't explain the technology — we show the result and let the contrast do the work. Secondary hook: airlines are rigging prices against you, and your agent is immune.
