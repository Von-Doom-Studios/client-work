## 1. Retention-Spike Reel Hook Engine

**Use for:** Short-form video (X, YouTube Shorts, TikTok, Reels)

**Principle:** The first 3 seconds determine whether someone watches or scrolls. High-contrast, contrarian, or curiosity-gap hooks outperform generic openers.

**Hook formulas for BRIJ:**
- **Contrarian:** "Airlines don't want you to know this."
- **Result-first:** "I just booked a flight without opening a single website."
- **Speed:** "30 seconds. Real flight. No tabs."
- **Curiosity gap:** "This is what happens when your AI agent books your flight."
- **Pain point:** "You search for flights on 3 sites and the price goes UP. Here's why."

**Rules:**
- Show the result or the hook in the first 3 seconds. Never start with setup.
- One visual per beat — don't let any frame sit for more than 3 seconds.
- Text on screen: 5 words max per frame.
- End with a clear action: "Try it at travel.brij.fi" or "Link in bio."

## 2. Save-Weighted Content Matrix

**Use for:** Instagram (Phase 2+), YouTube, X bookmarks

**Principle:** Platform algorithms weight saves and shares higher than likes. Content that people save for later or share with others gets 3–5x more distribution than content that just gets liked.

**High-save content types for BRIJ:**
- **How-to threads:** "How to book flights with your AI agent (step by step)"
- **Comparison graphics:** Traditional booking vs. BRIJ (side by side)
- **Cheat sheets:** "5 prompts that get the best flight prices"
- **Data reveals:** "We analyzed 1,000 agent bookings. Here's what we found."

**Rules:**
- If someone would screenshot it → it should be a save-optimized post.
- Include "Save this for later" or "Bookmark this" as natural CTAs where appropriate.
- Format for scannability: numbered lists, bold key points, visual hierarchy.

## 3. Growth-to-Cashflow Velocity Diagnostic

**Use for:** Weekly social performance audits (see [08-MILESTONES.md](../08-MILESTONES.md))

**Diagnostic rubric:**

| Signal | Diagnosis | Fix |
|--------|-----------|-----|
| High reach + low profile clicks | Content is interesting but doesn't connect to BRIJ | Stronger branding in posts, clearer CTAs |
| High profile clicks + flat followers | Profile/bio isn't converting | Update bio, pin best tweet, add clear value prop |
| High followers + low link clicks | Audience is growing but not converting | More CTAs to brij.fi/travel, better landing page |
| High link clicks + low signups | Landing page isn't converting | Fix landing page CTA, reduce friction |
| High signups + low bookings | Product friction or trust gap | Improve onboarding, add social proof, testimonials |

**Cadence:** Run this diagnostic weekly as part of Friday content review.
