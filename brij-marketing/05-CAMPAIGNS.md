## Campaign anatomy (every campaign uses this)

Every campaign in this file fills in every field below. If a field can't be filled in, the campaign is not ready to ship.

- **Name and number**
- **Objective:** the business outcome this drives
- **Dates:** start, end, key milestones in between
- **Audience:** which of the 4 from [02-STRATEGY.md](./02-STRATEGY.md) is primary
- **X posts:** linked entries in [06-CONTENT-LIBRARY.md](./06-CONTENT-LIBRARY.md). Specify whether each is product, comedy, or proof/POV.
- **Newsletter tie-in:** the specific newsletter issue and section that amplifies this campaign in long form. Required, not optional. The newsletter is the long-form version of the X posts. A campaign is not complete until it has a newsletter slot.
- **Owner:** lead agent or person.
- **Success metrics:** what we measure and the threshold.
- **Budget:** paid spend (linked to [03-BUDGET.md](./03-BUDGET.md))
- **Retro:** post-campaign review with what worked, what didn't, what we'd do differently. Logged inline at the bottom of the campaign entry.

## How X posts and the newsletter integrate

Each campaign produces both. The X posts deliver the hook in three short bursts across the week. The newsletter issue takes the same campaign and gives it the long-form treatment: full context, real numbers when available, links back to the X posts so subscribers can quote-post or reply, and a CTA tied to the campaign objective.

The rule:

- **Campaign starts on X.** The three X posts ship in the campaign week per [04-CALENDAR.md](./04-CALENDAR.md).
- **Newsletter follows within 30 days.** The next issue of 402 Receipts (see [08-NEWSLETTER.md](./08-NEWSLETTER.md)) carries the campaign in its "What shipped" lead and links to the original X posts.
- **Cross-link both ways.** X posts link to brij.fi with the campaign UTM. The newsletter issue links to the X post URLs.

A campaign that runs on X but never makes the newsletter is incomplete.
