## CRISIS MANAGEMENT

### If Referral System Breaks
- **Immediate:** Manual tracking in spreadsheet
- **Communication:** Email users explaining delay + bonus credits for inconvenience
- **Fix Timeline:** 48 hours maximum
- **Prevention:** Test system with team weekly

### If Content Backfires
- **Response:** Quick, honest acknowledgment if wrong
- **Delete:** Remove if factually incorrect
- **Learn:** Document what went wrong for future content guidelines
- **Pivot:** Shift to more conservative content temporarily

### If Booking System Has Issues
- **Transparency:** Immediate status page update
- **Communication:** Email all affected users with timeline
- **Compensation:** Automatic credits for inconvenience
- **Escalation:** All hands on fixing within 4 hours
