## Ground Rules

**1. The manifesto is the source of truth.**
BRIJ's manifesto defines the product, the architecture, and the conviction. Everything in this folder serves the manifesto's vision.

**2. The website is the product.**
brij.fi and travel.brij.fi are live. Marketing drives people to real, working products. Never promise what isn't shipped.

**3. Social media and email are the primary execution channels.**
This strategy exists to grow BRIJ's social presence and email list. Every document ultimately serves that goal.

**4. Milestones are non-negotiable.**
See [08-MILESTONES.md](./08-MILESTONES.md). We are building toward 10,000 email signups and 2,000–5,000 social followers. Every action is measured against those targets.

**5. Consistency over perfection.**
The #1 weakness identified by the team: inconsistent communication. Three months of silence on X. The cure is a weekly calendar and daily execution, not perfect content.

**6. Know which phase you're in.**
Phase 1 targets developers/geeks. Phase 2 targets early adopters. Phase 3 targets mainstream. The audience, message, and channel depend on the active phase. Check [05-GO-TO-MARKET.md](./05-GO-TO-MARKET.md) before producing anything.
