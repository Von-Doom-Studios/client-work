## Ground rules (non-negotiable)

1. **Senior bar.** Every artifact is something a founder, agent, or new hire could execute the same day. No memos.
2. **GitHub is the documentation system.** Not Notion. Not Google Docs.
3. **One file per concern.** Cross-reference; don't duplicate.
4. **Every file has a header.** Title, owner, last updated.
5. **Update on change, same day.**
6. **Voice is dry, technical, confident.** Builders trust receipts, not takes. No "excited to announce." No rocket emojis. No em dashes. No insider jargon a normal X user won't recognize.
7. **No token narrative in marketing.**
8. **X-only.** No LinkedIn, YouTube, TikTok, IG, Farcaster.
9. **External outreach goes through Ops.**
10. **Visuals go through VD-Creative.**
