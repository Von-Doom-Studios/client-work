## Cadence (set by [02-STRATEGY.md](./02-STRATEGY.md))

- **X:** 3 posts per week. Mon (product post), Wed (comedy), Fri (proof/POV).
- **Newsletter:** monthly, first Wednesday.

This is the cap. No surge weeks, no bonus posts. Anything not on this calendar does not ship.

## Slot rhythm

- **Monday:** product/feature post. Leads the week's theme.
- **Wednesday:** comedy post. Punches at incumbents or at industry absurdity, stays in-thesis. See comedy rules in [02-STRATEGY.md](./02-STRATEGY.md).
- **Friday:** proof, POV, or receipt. Reinforces the week's theme with a sharper edge.
- **First Wednesday of the month:** newsletter issue ships in the AM, before the comedy post in the PM.

## Status legend

- **DRAFT** - copy written, not yet reviewed
- **APPROVED** - reviewed and approved
- **POSTED** - live on the channel
- **HOLD** - blocked on dependency (creative asset, review, external)
